Introduction to Data Science & Artificial Intelligence

A five-day journey for anyone who must evaluate AI proposals, ask the right procurement questions, classify data correctly under PDPL & NDMO, and identify AI opportunities in their own work.

🌍 Bilingual (EN / عربي) 🎓 No coding required

Five days, told as one journey. Day 1 grounds you in what AI is — the foundations that have been with computer science since its dawn. Day 2 shows what you can do with AI today when much of the coding is no longer yours to do. Day 3 walks toward agents, and how to use them safely. Day 4 opens the hood — the building blocks (search, knowledge, learning, generation) that an agent reasons with. Day 5 puts those pieces together: you compose a simple agent of your own, on a problem you actually own.
