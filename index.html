AI for Leaders

For public-sector leaders who must evaluate AI proposals, ask the right procurement questions, classify data correctly under PDPL & NDMO, and identify AI opportunities in their own work.

🌍 Bilingual (EN / عربي) 🎓 No coding required

This is not a course about how to write AI code. It is a course about how to direct AI — judging when to use it, what data is safe to feed it, what to demand from technical teams, and where in your own work it can help most. By Day 5 you will have drafted a one-page AI opportunity proposal grounded in your real responsibilities.
